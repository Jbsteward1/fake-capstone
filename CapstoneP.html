ABOUT US

IT'S ALL ABOUT ME

It's All About Me is a selfie studio where people from our community and all over the world are able to take interactive and creative pictures. The stations are uniquely designed for people of all ages to enjoy.



I AAM is an acronym derived from the name of our studio. As some of us may know, "I Am" is placed in front of positive affirmations to uplift and change our mindset for the better. Not only do we want you to leave with great pictures; we also want you to feel uplifted and positive when you exit our establishment!
